overview

The PSU Hotel is modern, elegant 5-star hotel overlooking see, perfect for a romantic, charming vacation, in the enchanting setting of Lingayen Beach. While you enjoy a cocktail by the swimming pool on the rooftop terrace, you will be stunned by the breathtaking view of Lingayen Beach. The rooms at the PSU Hotel are all new, well-lit and inviting. Our reception staff will be happy to help you during your stay in Pangasinan, suggesting itineraries, guided visits and some good restaurant in the historic centre.
